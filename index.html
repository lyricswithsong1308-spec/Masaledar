In Bihar, many families prefer cooking without onion and garlic. With Yujanii Garam Masala, you can still enjoy delicious chole full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup boiled chickpeas (chole)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tsp ginger paste
  • 2 tsp Yujanii Garam Masala
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Method

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds.
  2. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  3. Add ginger paste and stir well.
  4. Mix in Yujanii Garam Masala and salt.
  5. Add boiled chickpeas, cook for 5â€“10 minutes, garnish with coriander leaves.
